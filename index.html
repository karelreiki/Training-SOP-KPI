7 CRITERIA FOR GOOD KPIs

1
Kuantitatif

Measurable in objective values ‚Äî e.g., Cost Reduction 10%

2
Mudah Dipahami

Shows at a glance what is measured ‚Äî e.g., Defect Ratio

3
Mendorong Perilaku

Drives desired behavior ‚Äî e.g., # of rule violations

4
Visible

Impact directly visible on results ‚Äî e.g., Complaint resolution speed

5
Komitmen Bersama

Defined & agreed by all process participants ‚Äî e.g., On-time delivery

6
Mengukur Yang Penting

Only measure what matters for end results ‚Äî e.g., Sales target calculations

7
Seimbang

Balance between productivity, efficiency, and cost

GOOD KPIs = Actionable + Measurable + Purposeful

Simple

Use Case

Straightforward, Small scope

  • Statement of purpose
  • Summary or overview
  • Step-by-step instructions
  • 3‚Äì5 sections
  • DRI (Directly Responsible Individual)

Hierarchical

Use Case

  • Statement of purpose
  • Table of contents
